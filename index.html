First post : ( Do not show your imperfections!)

This post is written by anonymous.

It can take you time to love your body.Now, do not you love your legs? Wear jeans or longer skirts.Do not you like your arms? Wear a nice sweater or a jacket which hide them.Wear it as long as you learn to love your body. Remember - you should learn it!

second post : ( Ask people around you!)

This post is written by anonymous.

Do not you like your eyes because they are not blue? Your mom loves them because they are like chocolate. Are your legs too long? Your best friend wants to have your legs because you are like a model. Sit for a while with people you like and who know you. Listen to them. They can be a mirror to you. They know you well. They can tell you why you are lovely and special.

Top sidebar

Learn from celebrities!They are actors/actress not only in films, but also at interviews, premieres and gala evenings. Watch videos with them and learn from them. How do they walk? How do they talk? make notes in life.

Middle sidebar

Love yourself! It is easy to say it but difficult to do.Take your time. Do not look to 'mirrors and shops' windows.You do not have to control yourself. Tell yourself that you are nice! And believe it!

Bottom sidebar

Hide your flaws! It was already suggested to show your perfections. You can hide them if it makes you feel comfortable. However,do not forget that even the imperfections are parts of you. You are hiding them until you learn how to accept and love yourself.
